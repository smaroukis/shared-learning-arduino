**Boolean Operators**
- `&&`
- `||` 
- `!`

**Arithmetic**

**Compound Operators**
- `i++`: first increments `i` by one and then evaluates it
- `++i`: first evaluates `i` then increments
- `+=`
- `-=`
- `*=`
- `/=`

**Bitwise Operators** 
- `&` (bitwise and)
- `<<` (bitshift left) see [bit-shifting](bit-shifting.md)
- `>>` (bitshift right)
- `^` (bitwise xor)
- `|` (bitwise or)
- `~` (bitwise not)
