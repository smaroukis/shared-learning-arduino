# RLC Filter Summary
![[attachments/LAoE/RLC circuit.png]]
Resonant frequency: 
- $\omega_0=\frac{1}{\sqrt{LC}}$ or $f_0=\frac{1}{2\pi \sqrt{LC}}$.

Q factor:
- $Q=\frac{f_0}{\Delta f}=2 \pi N_{1/e}$

Design Choices:
- high R: high Q & less energy out
- low R: low Q & more energy out
